How Secure is Mega Cloud Storage?

End to End Encryption

MEGA employs a zero-knowledge encryption policy. This means even MEGA does not store your encrypted data. It is you and only you who have access to and can unlock your data, thus protecting it from security attacks.

Two Factor Authentication

2FA adds an additional layer of security to your already secure MEGA account. When enabled, it sends you a unique token to your preferred device, which you have to reproduce to confirm your identity.

Secure Link Sharing

You can secure your shared links with decryptions keys. And disclose them to the intended recipient via a different channel of your choice. MEGA allows you to do this which in turn ensures your shared links stay secure.

Anti Ransomware Protection

MEGA’s robust backup recovery system ensures your devices stay clear of ransomware infections. This also means the system can take your infected MEGA account back in time to a moment when it was uninfected and resume things like nothing happened.
